Our Story

Our project was born thanks to the meeting between Marco Lastella and Simone Colombaretti.
The encounter between the Funk and soul culture of the two artists allowed the birth of a new way of conceiving music. Our songs talk about dreams, passions and  stories that come from the heart, and that's what we want to share every day with our fans.
