Marco Lastella

He's a singer and songwriter in Milan
After a full immersion of studies of his voice and severals gigs he decides to create a new Funk/Soul/RnB live band.
Thanks to the meeting with simone Colobaretti, the bass player, founded a musical project that really captures the attention of the underground scene.

He is currently working on the release of his first single, followed by an album.
All tracks are original with some rearranged covers.
